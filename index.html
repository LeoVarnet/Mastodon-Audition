Audition scientists on Mastodon

A list of academics working in psychoacoustics, psycholinguistics, audiology, and auditory modeling.

You want to get in touch with audition scientists from around the world on Mastodon? This page lists accounts from people and institutions working on audition and offers an easy method to follow multiple accounts at once. Just decide if you want to follow all or only some of the accounts listed below. You are provided with a .csv-file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow all accounts included. Here you can find a short instruction, on how to do this.

If you want to connect to your account and directly follow people from this list or other disciplines, you can also use Mark Igra’s interface for finding Academics on Mastodon.

Further information on how to be added or removed can be found at the bottom of this page.

Clicking on a keyword or language allows you to filter the list; clicking the activated keyword/language again will undo the filter.

Accounts by handle, name, keywords, and main language(s) (most recent first)

You want to be on the list, too? Please fill out this Form (preferred), or send me a direct message on Mastodon: @leovarnet@qoto.org

You are interested in audition-related content on mastodon, but you don’t want to be added to the list? Then you could consider following the hashtags #pschoacoustics, #audition or #psycholinguistics.

If you want to be removed from the list, please contact me directly: @leovarnet@qoto.org.
